Business Proposal: Car Wash & Valet Services
1. Executive Summary

This proposal outlines the establishment of a modern car wash and valet service designed to provide convenient, high-quality vehicle cleaning to customers in a high-traffic location. The business will focus on speed, affordability, repeat customers, and customer loyalty, supported by an online booking and rewards system.

The car wash will cater to private vehicle owners, fleet clients, and local businesses, offering tiered wash packages and optional premium services.
